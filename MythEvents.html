Game Events

Card Games

You can Join your fellow planeswalkers, Pokemon Trainers, Duelists, etc. in an all-out Card Brawl, including card games such as Magic the Gathering, Pokémon TCG and Yu Gi Yo!

MTG and Yu Gi Yo! Tournaments

We also host a MTG and Yu Gi Yo! Tournaments on their respected days. So make sure you get together your best decks and Compete to be the Best, whether it's with counterspells or Dark Magician

Wargames

Take your minitures and tactics and take the battle against other Warmongers in Wargames!!!

Model Painting Contest

You can take your model painting skills and put them to the test with the Model Painting Contest, where other members will make the best painted model possible and they will be judged by our lovely judges.

DMasterclass

Ever wanted to become a DM in your very own DnD campaign but don't know how? The DMasterclass is made up of Myth's most experienced DMs and will teach some tips and tricks you can learn to use in your next campaign!

Dungeon Legends

Dungeon Legends has several DnD campaigns which you can participate in, whether you're there from the very start or just dropping in mid-way to make a cameo and leave quicker than a mage using teleportation because YOU can choose when to play!

Oneshot Contest

The Oneshot Contest will challenge your ability as a DM to create an immersive and captivating campaign that will be played in one singular sitting. Think you got what it takes to create a great story in one session?

Board Games

Feel like hunting down Dracula as Van helsing in 'Fury of Dracula', maybe even dive into Diplomacy, Conflict and Conquest in a game of 'Risk' or just a mental game of Chess with a friend, a night of 'Board Games' sounds perfect for you!

Dungeons and Drag Queens

To all you members of the LGBT community, we have a DnD session going on exclusively for you! So if you wish to socialise with other LGBT members and destroy goblins with a level 3 fireball, go right ahead!

Society Events

Re-Welcome Fair

The Re-Welcome Fair will re-welcome back any previous members from the previous years with a wide range of activities. Perfect for a fun day with your party!

Society Picture Day

Want to put your stamp in the society's history? Come along and get your picture taken for all to see!

Extra Events to look forward to

RPG Quiz

Think you got the RPG knowledge to parttake in this quiz? Then come prove it in the RPG Quiz and claim the crown of the RPG Wizard!

LARP Trip

Come along on a one-day trip to the Live Action Roleplay event, run by jestering fools and heroes. Everyone will be split into players and the other half into monsters, then they will swap for the second game. For more information, visit the Myth Society Social Media or ask one of the organisers.

Pub Trip

Do you think you are the ultimate fantasy know-it-all, join us for the Pub Trip and come prove your worth!

Egg Quest

A Special Easter-Themed Oneshot campaign with bunnies as enemies and easter eggs that has special buffs and perks. I wouldn't want to miss this one-time event for sure!

You can ask any admin about any extra info, they don't bite ^-^
